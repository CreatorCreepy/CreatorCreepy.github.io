The controls in geometry dash are very simple. There are two ways to control your charecter: Taping, and Taping and holding down.

Here is a fancy chart to show how you control each gamemode:

Image Gamemode Control
Cube Tap to jump, Hold to jump multiple times
Ship Hold to go up, let go to go down
Ball Tap to flip gravity
Ufo Tap to jump in midair
Wave Hold to go up, let go to go down
Robot Hold for a big jump, jump is based on length of hold
Spider Tap to change gravity, quickly
