The objects in Geometry Dash are what make up the entire level and makes ot fun to play, Levels can be simple or very complex.

The chart below will explain what each type of object does:

Image Object Name Description
Spike The spike is the main object you are trying to avoid. Contact will end your attempt!
Block What makes up most of the gameplay!
Saw Just like a spike, the saws will end your attempt. They spin! 8)
Decoration Geometry Dash has many objects to decorate levels. These do not affect the gameplay in any way and are just there fpor astetic purposes.
